# Branding Options

## Recommended

- **Vistora**
  - easy to understand function
  - neutral enough for B2B and consumer channels
  - works for API, product UI, and publication context

## Alternatives

- **UnmosaicLab**
- **ClearPatch**
- **AegisRestore**
- **FluxClean**
- **KagamiFlow**

## Naming checklist

- short and pronounceable
- no heavy legal risk keywords
- easy Telegram handle naming
- flexible for future non-AV restoration use cases
